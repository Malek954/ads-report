IMPACT·SYSTEMS · Amenaa - Monthly Marketing Plan

July & August Plan

Why this plan - 4 facts from the data

  • Linen Shirt is the hero right now - 22-43x return on its ads, the best in the account. Shirt Dress is #1 by revenue (EGP 146K in 30 days). Push both hard.
  • Catalog / Best-Seller ads are the strongest ad type - 11-26x return. One runs every week, always.
  • Buyers shop midday (11am-5pm) and 9pm, and Wednesday & Saturday are the strongest days - every launch and new ad goes live on those windows.
  • The account was OFF last July-August - so this plan follows the Egyptian summer calendar: Sahel season is peaking now, back-to-school lands late August.
JulyTheme: Sahel summer peak · push the winners, no discounts needed
Week 1-2 Jul 1 - 14
Push winners · Full price
  • Scale the proven winners: Linen Shirt, Shirt Dress, Maxi Denim Dress - returns are 12-43x, there is zero reason to discount.
  • Lead with the Sahel / summer light edit - breathable fabrics, beach-to-street modest looks.
  • Every ad says الدفع عند الاستلام (COD) + free shipping over EGP 2,500 - the two trust levers that close Egyptian buyers.
Week 3 Jul 15 - 22
New arrivals drop
  • Mid-summer new-arrivals drop (no discount - newness is the offer). Go live Saturday Jul 18, 1 pm - the strongest day and the midday buying peak.
  • Retarget the last 30 days of visitors with the new pieces + free-shipping reminder.
Week 4 Jul 23 - 31
Revolution Day flash · 15%
  • Jul 23 (Revolution Day) flash offer - 3 days only (Jul 23-25): 15% on selected styles + free shipping. Short and scarce, so the brand never looks discount-driven.
  • Back to full price Jul 26 and keep scaling winners into August.

5 rules for the owner

  1. Never discount the winners. Linen Shirt and Shirt Dress sell at full price - the returns prove it.
  2. Maximum 2 short offer windows in 2 months. Amenaa is mid-market, not a discount brand - customers must never learn to wait for sales.
  3. COD + free shipping over EGP 2,500 appear in every single ad. They are the #1 trust and AOV levers in Egypt.
  4. Launch everything Saturday or Wednesday, 11am-1pm. That is when Egyptian women actually buy.
  5. Judge results on week-old numbers, never the last 2-3 days (they always look low before purchases finish counting).
