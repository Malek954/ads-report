July & August Plan

Why this plan — 4 facts from the data

  • Mid-July is the danger zone. Last year sales crashed Jul 8–21 (return dropped to 1.5×) then recovered strongly from Jul 22. We place the sale exactly there.
  • End of August is the biggest week of the year. Last year Aug 26–31 made the most revenue of any week (4.9× return). That's when the new collection launches.
  • "Best-Seller" carousel ads are the strongest thing running now — 6–7.5× return, double everything else. We run one every week.
  • الدوامات (swirl-print) is July's winning style with competitors — Bayan needs its own version in front of customers now.
JulyTheme: Summer lightweight + الدوامات · beat the mid-month dip
Week 1–2 Jul 1 – 14
Push winners · No discount
  • Push the proven winners at full price: FJ (top seller), Soft Olive, Best-Sellers carousel.
  • Launch Bayan's الدوامات (swirl) pieces — it's the style winning in Kuwait right now. Feature fabric movement in video.
  • Message: خامات صيفية خفيفة — light summer fabrics, travel-friendly.
Week 3 Jul 15 – 21
Mid-Summer Sale · 15–20%
  • This was last year's weakest week — don't fight it, convert it: “تخفيضات منتصف الصيف” on selected summer styles only (never sitewide). Start it Wednesday Jul 15, 8 pm (a strong day carries the announcement).
  • 15–20% off slower styles + last season pieces. Winners (FJ, Soft Olive) stay full price.
  • Retarget everyone who visited in the last 30 days with the sale.
Week 4 Jul 22 – 31
Back to full price · Scale
  • End the sale on time — last year demand jumped from Jul 22 on its own (4.4× return). Scale budget on winners.
  • Tease: “قريباً — مجموعة الخريف” (autumn collection coming) to build anticipation.

5 rules for the owner

  1. Never discount the winners. FJ and Soft Olive sell at full price — discounts go to slow styles only.
  2. Only 2 sale windows in 2 months. More sales = customers learn to wait. Bayan sells newness & quality.
  3. Every week needs fresh content (2 videos + 1 carousel). Summer ad costs are the year's highest — old creative dies fast.
  4. The autumn launch (Aug 23) is the most important date of the summer. Everything in August builds toward it.
  5. Judge results on week-old numbers, never on the last 2–3 days (they always look low before purchases finish counting).
